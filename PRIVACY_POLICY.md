Privacy Policy for Desert Rally

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

1. Introduction
Welcome to Desert Rally ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game on the CrazyGames platform.

2. Information We Collect
To provide essential game features, such as the global leaderboard and progression tracking, we collect the following data:

Username / Display Name: We access your CrazyGames account username through the CrazyGames SDK. If you play as a guest, we automatically generate a generic guest tag (e.g., Player#123456).

Player ID: We generate a unique, anonymous identifier (UUID) stored locally on your device to track your returning sessions.

Gameplay Data: We collect your in-game performance metrics, including scores (distance traveled), top speeds, average speeds, selected vehicles, and levels.

3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data strictly for the following purposes:

Leaderboards: To display your username, selected car, and high score on the public global leaderboard.

Game Progression: To save your high scores, unlocked vehicles, and stats.

Anti-Cheat System: To analyze gameplay physics (e.g., speed and distance anomalies) to detect and prevent cheating on the leaderboards.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is only shared with the following essential services:

CrazyGames: Our game is hosted on CrazyGames, and we utilize their SDK for user authentication and advertisements. Please refer to the CrazyGames Privacy Policy for how they handle your data.

Backend & Database Hosting: Game scores and usernames are securely transmitted to and stored on our cloud database (MongoDB) solely to operate the leaderboard system.

5. Data Retention & Deletion
We retain your leaderboard data (username and score) as long as it is necessary to maintain the game's competitive features. If you wish to have your data removed from our leaderboard database, please contact us at pudingearl@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy
Our game does not knowingly collect personal, identifiable information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.
